My name is Sean Escriva. I'm an infrastructure hacker and continuous delivery evangelist with over 20 years of IT operations experience and 15 years with AWS and cloud services.

My past work and experiences are easily found online, if you're
interested, or just feel free to [reach out](https://hachyderm.io/@webframp).
I've spent years building networks, managing data centers, building
infrastructure and teaching DevOps.

I have a lot of history using [Chef](https://www.chef.io/), formerly worked with
the amazingly talented teams at [Heavy Water Software](http://hw-ops.com) and [Sensu](https://sensu.io/).

Currently I'm using Go, learning Rust and always trying to get better with Swift. I maintain [MobileOrg](https://github.com/MobileOrg) and perpetually learn from others.

My limited spare time is filled with functional languages, mechanical keyboards,
occasional attempts at guitar and learning spoken languages.

#### Webframp?
It started as a joke. My wife's side of the family didn't use the word
*fart* and instead used *framp*. I always found this hilarious so
made up webframp as a joke and a reminder to never take myself or
anything online too seriously.
